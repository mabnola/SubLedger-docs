Downloadable reports let you export a structured summary of your subscriptions — useful for expense reviews, tax preparation, or sharing with a finance professional. Reports are available on the **Business plan** ($50/month).

<Info>
  If you're on the Free, Personal, or Family plan, you'll need to upgrade to Business to access downloadable reports. See [compare plans](/getting-started/billing/compare-plans) for a full feature breakdown.
</Info>

## What reports include

Each report covers the subscriptions in your account at the time of export. You can expect a report to contain:

- Service name and description
- Billing amount and cycle (monthly or annual)
- Current status (Active, Renewing soon)
- Monthly cost equivalent for each subscription
- Total monthly and yearly spend summary

## How to download a report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the dashboard">
    Sign in to SubLedger at [app.mysubledger.com](https://app.mysubledger.com) and go to your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Reports">
    Select **Reports** from the dashboard navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download your report">
    Click **Download report**. The file will save to your device immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Bookkeeper access

The Business plan also includes **bookkeeper access**, which lets you invite a bookkeeper or accountant to view your subscription data directly — no need to forward files manually.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Account settings">
    Open **Settings** from the top navigation in SubLedger.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Bookkeeper access section">
    Scroll to the **Bookkeeper access** section within your account settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite your bookkeeper">
    Enter your bookkeeper's email address and click **Send invite**. They'll receive an email with instructions to access your reports.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Bookkeeper access grants view-only access to your subscription data and reports. Your bookkeeper cannot edit or delete subscriptions on your behalf.
</Warning>
