SubLedger's free tier is permanently free — no trials, no feature cliffs, no pressure to upgrade. If you find SubLedger useful and want to support its continued development, you can make a voluntary contribution from the pricing page.

## How it works

The supporter option appears on the **Free plan** card on the [pricing page](https://app.mysubledger.com/pricing). You choose:

- **Recurring (monthly)** — an ongoing monthly contribution that you can cancel at any time.
- **One-time** — a single contribution with no recurring charges.

Set any dollar amount you'd like — the minimum is $1. SubLedger processes contributions securely through Stripe, the same payment provider used for all paid plans.

## How to contribute

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the pricing page">
    Sign in at [app.mysubledger.com](https://app.mysubledger.com) and navigate to **Pricing**, or visit the pricing page directly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the supporter section">
    Under the Free plan card, look for the **Pay what you can** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your amount and frequency">
    Select **Recurring** or **One-time**, then enter the dollar amount you'd like to contribute.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    Click the contribute button to proceed to Stripe checkout. Enter your payment details and confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Contributing does not change your plan or unlock additional features. It's a way to support SubLedger while continuing to use the free tier.
</Note>

## Managing your contribution

If you set up a recurring contribution and want to cancel or adjust it, you can manage it through the Stripe customer portal. Contact [help@mysubledger.com](mailto:help@mysubledger.com) if you need help.
