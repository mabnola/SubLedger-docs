SubLedger offers four plans built around different use cases, from personal subscription tracking to full business expense management. All plans are grounded in the same privacy-first approach: your data is never sold or used for advertising.

<Note>
  You can start with the free tier immediately — no credit card required. Upgrade whenever you're ready.
</Note>

## Free

The Free plan is permanently free and gives you everything you need to take control of your recurring charges.

  <Card title="Up to 10 subscriptions" icon="list">
    Add and track up to 10 recurring charges.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Renewal reminders" icon="bell">
    Receive email notifications before each subscription renews.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dashboard & summaries" icon="chart-bar">
    View monthly and yearly spend estimates across all your subscriptions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="No credit card required" icon="credit-card">

## Personal — $10/month

The Personal plan is designed for individuals who have more subscriptions to manage or who submit expenses for employer reimbursement.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Up to 20 subscriptions" icon="list">
    Double the subscription limit of the free tier.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Everything in Free" icon="check">
    Renewal reminders, dashboard, and spend summaries included.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Employer reimbursement tracking" icon="briefcase">
    Mark subscriptions as reimbursable and track outstanding amounts from your employer.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  If you pay for work tools like Figma, Notion, or cloud storage and submit those costs for reimbursement, the Personal plan's reimbursement tracking can save you time on expense reports.
</Tip>
