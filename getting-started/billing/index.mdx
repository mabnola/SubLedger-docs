SubLedger offers four plans — Free, Personal, Family, and Business — so you can start at no cost and scale up as your needs change. All paid plans are available on monthly or annual billing, and you can upgrade or downgrade at any time.

## Pricing at a glance

| Plan | Monthly price | Subscriptions | Standout feature |
|------|--------------|---------------|-----------------|
| Free | $0 | Up to 10 | Renewal reminders, dashboard |
| Personal | $10/mo | Up to 20 | Employer reimbursement tracking |
| Family | $20/mo | Up to 50 | Shared family cost tracking |
| Business | $50/mo | Unlimited | Bookkeeper access, downloadable reports |

<Tip>
  Choosing annual billing saves approximately 17% compared to paying month-to-month.
</Tip>

<Note>
  SubLedger never sells your data or uses your subscription information for ad targeting, regardless of which plan you're on.
</Note>
