Your data belongs to you. SubLedger is built on a privacy-first foundation — your subscription information is never sold to third parties, and you have full control to export or delete it at any time.

## SubLedger's privacy commitment

SubLedger collects only the information needed to operate the service: your subscription details, billing amounts, and account preferences. That data is used to power your dashboard and deliver renewal reminders — nothing more.

**SubLedger never sells your data.** Your subscription habits, spending patterns, and personal information are not shared with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party for commercial purposes.

<Note>
  For the complete details of how SubLedger collects, uses, and protects your data, read the [SubLedger privacy policy](/support/privacy-policy).
</Note>

## Exporting your data

You can download a copy of your SubLedger data at any time. This export includes your subscription list, billing details, and account information in a portable format.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Account settings">
    Sign in at [app.mysubledger.com](https://app.mysubledger.com), then navigate to **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the Data section">
    Scroll to the **Data** section within your account settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request your export">
    Click **Export my data**. SubLedger will prepare your export and either download it immediately or send it to your account email address.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Exporting your data before you cancel is a good way to keep a personal record of the subscriptions you were tracking.
</Tip>

## Deleting your account

If you want to stop using SubLedger, you can permanently delete your account. Deleting your account removes all of your data from SubLedger's systems.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Account settings">
    Navigate to **Settings** from the top navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the Data section">
    Scroll to the **Data** section in your settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete your account">
    Click **Delete account** and confirm when prompted. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Account deletion is irreversible. All of your subscriptions, spend history, and account data will be permanently removed. Export your data first if you want to keep a copy.
</Warning>
