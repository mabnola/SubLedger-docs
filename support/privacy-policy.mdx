## Summary

    To operate the service, process transactions, improve site performance, and send you information about SubLedger features and updates. Usage data is also used to analyze site trends.

## Your rights

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="California residents — SB 27 rights" icon="map-pin">
    Under California Law SB 27, if you are a California resident you have the right to receive — once per year — information about any third parties with whom we have shared information about you or your family for their marketing purposes during the previous calendar year.

    To make this request, send an email to [help@mysubledger.com](mailto:help@mysubledger.com) with **"California Privacy Request"** in the subject line.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Children's privacy — COPPA" icon="shield">
    SubLedger is a general-audience site. Consistent with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), we do not knowingly collect any information from, or sell to, children under the age of 13.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Opting out of Google Analytics" icon="bar-chart-2">
    SubLedger uses Google Analytics to understand how the site is used. If you prefer not to be tracked by Google Analytics, you can opt out using the [Google Analytics Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

Personal Information. "Personal Information" includes any information regarding a natural person that may be used directly to identify the person. Personal Information that we collect may vary with each separate purpose for which you provide it, and it may include one or more of the following categories: name, physical address, an email address, phone number, credit card information including credit card number, expiration date, and billing address, and location data.

Personal Information. We collect Personal Information at the time you provide it to us through sign-up forms and as part of your registration for an account, product, or service, or promotion from this website. Personal Information may vary with each sign-up or registration.

Usage Data. We reserve the right to monitor your use of this site. As you navigate through this site, Usage Data may be passively collected (that is, gathered without your actively providing the information) using various analytics and reporting technologies, such as cookies and web beacons.

We reserve the right to make full use of Usage Data. For example, we may use Usage Data to provide better service to site visitors, customize the site based on your preferences, compile and analyze statistics and trends about the use of this site, and otherwise administer and improve this site for your use.
